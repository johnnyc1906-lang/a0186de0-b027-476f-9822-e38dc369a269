Our Story

A Fusion of Flavor,
Culture & Energy

Mexcla Restaurant is Newtown's premier destination where bold, authentic cuisine meets an electrifying nightlife atmosphere. Nestled in the heart of 48 S Main Street, we've crafted a space where every visit is a sensory celebration.

Our culinary team crafts every dish with passion and precision — from vibrant small plates and signature entrees to creative cocktails that tell a story. Whether you're joining us for an intimate dinner or a night of live music and dancing, Mexcla delivers an experience unlike any other.

Warmly rated 4.5 stars by our community of 82+ loyal guests, we're proud to be Newtown's go-to spot for great food, great drinks, and unforgettable memories.

4 Experiences
What We Offer

Four Distinct Experiences Under One Roof

From refined dining to late-night revelry, Mexcla is your all-in-one destination for exceptional food, drinks, and atmosphere.

Fine Dining

Savor expertly crafted dishes made from fresh, high-quality ingredients. Our menu blends bold Latin-inspired flavors with modern culinary techniques for a dining experience that satisfies every palate.

Full Bar

Explore our curated cocktail menu featuring handcrafted drinks, premium spirits, fine wines, and local craft beers. Our skilled bartenders blend creativity with classic techniques to deliver your perfect pour.

Night Club

When the sun goes down, Mexcla transforms into an electrifying nightclub experience. Dance to curated DJ sets and live performances in an atmosphere buzzing with energy, music, and great company.

Private Events

Host your next celebration at Mexcla. From intimate gatherings to large private parties, our versatile space and dedicated event team ensure every detail is perfect for your special occasion.
